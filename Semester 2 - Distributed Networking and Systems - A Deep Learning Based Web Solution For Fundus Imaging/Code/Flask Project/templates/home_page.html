About Us

Our Proposed solution consists of a state of the art tensor flow deep neural network deployed at the backend. The Doctor logs into our website with his user account. After user registration and successful login, he is able to upload the fundusimages and get classification results to clearly classify the diabetic retinopathy cases on the go. This solution could also be utilized with an add-on lens that could be attached on mobile phone camera. Hence, diabetic retinopathy cases could be diagnosed by the users using our website on the go. The doctor will take the image and upload it. He will get back the classification with diagnosis. He will write further comments. The patient can login and see his results along with the comments.
